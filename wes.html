About Us

Our Vision & Mission

WES Luxembourg for Education is dedicated to empowering underserved communities in India through quality education. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for transforming lives and creating sustainable change.

Founded with a vision to bridge educational gaps, we work closely with local communities to develop programs that address their specific needs and challenges.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to quality education regardless of their socioeconomic background.

Our Mission

To provide innovative educational programs that empower students and transform communities.

Our Programs

Educational Initiatives

Discover our comprehensive range of programs designed to provide quality education and support to underserved communities.

CLAS Program

Our Comprehensive Learning and Support program provides quality education to children from underserved communities, focusing on core academic subjects and holistic development.

  • Core academic subjects
  • Personalized attention
  • Regular assessments

Computer Lab

Our computer labs provide students with access to technology and digital literacy skills essential for success in today's world.

  • Basic computer skills
  • Internet literacy
  • Educational software

Focused Study Group

Small group sessions that provide targeted academic support to students who need additional help in specific subjects.

  • Small group learning
  • Subject-specific support
  • Personalized attention

Mentorship Program

Connecting students with mentors who provide guidance, support, and inspiration for academic and personal growth.

  • One-on-one mentoring
  • Career guidance
  • Life skills development

Virtual Classes

Online learning sessions that connect students with expert teachers and resources from around the world.

  • Live online sessions
  • Expert guest teachers
  • Interactive learning

Dhanpuri Public School

Our flagship school providing quality education to children from underserved communities in Dhanpuri.

  • Comprehensive curriculum
  • Qualified teachers
  • Modern facilities
